How It Works
Simple.
Powerful.

Four steps from raw video to fully accessible, professionally encoded output.

01

Upload Your File

Drop any video or audio file — MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, MP3, WAV. Up to 500MB.

02

AI Extracts & Transcribes

Audio is extracted, normalized to 16kHz, and sent through Google Speech-to-Text for accurate recognition.

03

Generate Accessibility Layer

Sign clips are assembled and synced, or subtitles are translated and timed to the speech.

04

Download Encoded Output

Final H.264/AAC MP4 with side panel — or export a standalone .srt subtitle file.
